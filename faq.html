Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all tree care services. Just get in touch to schedule.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We’re fully licensed and insured to work throughout Colorado, giving you peace of mind on every job.

Can you remove trees near power lines or buildings?

Yes, we specialize in precision removals in tight or hazardous spaces. Our crew is trained in rigging and safety protocols.

Do I need a permit for tree removal?

In some areas of Lakewood or nearby cities, yes. We’ll help you determine if a permit is needed and assist with the paperwork if required.

What if I have a tree emergency?

Call us right away! We offer emergency tree services for storm damage or hazardous limbs. We’ll prioritize your safety and quick response.

Do you offer stump grinding?

Yes, stump grinding is available as an optional service with tree removals or as a standalone request.
